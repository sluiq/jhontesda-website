Chamantad-Tiñan, Viewpoint

The Chamantad-Tiñan Viewpoint is one of the most picturesque and iconic spots on Batan Island, located in the province of Batanes in the northernmost part of the Philippines. Known for its breathtaking landscapes and panoramic views, the Chamantad-Tiñan Viewpoint offers a glimpse of the raw and untouched beauty of the Batanes Islands.

Puerto Princesa, River National, Park

The Puerto Princesa Subterranean River National Park, commonly known as the Puerto Princesa Underground River, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the New 7 Wonders of Nature. It is located on the western coast of Palawan Island in the Philippines, approximately 80 kilometers north of Puerto Princesa City.
